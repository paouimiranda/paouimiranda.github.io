How to Delete Your Account

We understand that you may want to delete your Rubric account. This page explains the process for requesting account deletion and what happens to your data.

Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days of your request.

Step-by-Step Process

Follow these simple steps to request the deletion of your Rubric account:

1
Send a Deletion Request Email

Send an email to rubric.capstone@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Rubric account.

2
Include Required Information

In your email, include the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and provide your registered email address and username to help us identify your account.

3
Confirm Your Identity

We may ask you to verify your identity to ensure account security. This may include answering security questions or confirming recent account activity.

4
Receive Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation email within 5 business days acknowledging your deletion request.

5
Account Deletion Complete

Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request. You will receive a final confirmation once the process is complete.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently deleted from our servers:

Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, username, and password
  • Profile Data: Profile picture, bio, and all profile settings
  • Study Content: All notes, study materials, uploaded documents, and images
  • Learning Progress: Quiz results, study session history, and activity logs
  • User Preferences: App settings, customizations, and saved preferences
  • Account Metadata: Account creation date, last login, and usage statistics

Data That May Be Retained:

  • Legal Compliance: Minimal data required for legal, tax, or regulatory compliance (typically anonymized transaction records)
  • Security Logs: Anonymized security and fraud prevention logs for up to 90 days
  • Backup Data: Data in encrypted backups will be deleted according to our backup retention schedule (maximum 90 days)
Retention Period

All personal data will be completely removed from our active servers within 30 days of your deletion request. Data in backup systems will be purged within 90 days.

Your Rights Under Philippine Law

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), you have the following rights:

  • Right to be Forgotten: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a copy of your data before deletion
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your personal data
  • Right to File a Complaint: Lodge complaints with the National Privacy Commission
